# Veronica brand

## Core idea

**The agent thinks. Veronica connects.**

Veronica is quiet infrastructure between an agent harness and an explicitly exposed coding workspace. It should feel like a trusted operator: calm, exact, honest about authority, and absent until needed.

Veronica is not the intelligence in the system. It does not perform a personality, pretend to understand the user, or compete with the outer harness. It opens a narrow route to execution, enforces the local boundary, returns the result, and gets out of the way.

## Personality

- **Calm, not theatrical.** Powerful operations should feel controlled rather than dramatic.
- **Precise, not bureaucratic.** Say exactly what is exposed, where work runs, and which component owns a decision.
- **Capable, not autonomous.** Veronica executes requested work; it does not invent goals or act like another agent.
- **Protective, not paternal.** Boundaries should be explicit and locally enforced without pretending risk has disappeared.
- **Human-named, not human-pretending.** The name gives the system presence. The product should not imitate a person.

## Voice

Use short, direct sentences. Name trust boundaries and failure modes plainly. Prefer concrete verbs such as `expose`, `open`, `route`, `run`, `close`, and `verify`.

Avoid mystical language, cute bot copy, and vague claims such as “secure by default” or “AI magic.” Do not describe command filtering as a sandbox. Do not imply Veronica owns planning, reasoning, or user intent.

## Taglines

Primary:

> Quiet infrastructure for powerful agents.

Supporting:

> Expose a workspace. Keep authority local.

## The mark

The mark is a minimal `V` above a single point.

- The two descending strokes form a gateway or controlled channel.
- The point is the explicitly selected device or workspace where execution lands.
- The visible separation between them represents the handoff between planning and execution.
- The open construction avoids suggesting a sealed sandbox or an all-encompassing system.

The mark should remain monochrome, flat, and unembellished. Do not add robot faces, circuitry decoration, gradients, glow effects, or a shield unless a specific security feature—not the whole product—is being represented.

## Color

- Ink: `#111827`
- Night: `#0B1220`
- Paper: `#F8FAFC`
- Secondary text: `#94A3B8`

The core mark should normally appear in Ink on a light background or Paper on a dark background.
